Loop Desert is a game created in 48 hours for the 28th Ludum Dare Competition.

This is a game about choice, a hostile environment, and limited resources.

You must survive the Loop Desert trying to reach the city before you run out of water.

Choose wisely the right direction. You will find two bottles of water on your path to help you reach your destination, but only one can save your life.

Controls: right and left keys to move the explorer.

Tools: Crafty.js, Audacity, Draw!
