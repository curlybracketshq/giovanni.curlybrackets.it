Amortization Schedule

About this tool

Use this tool to get useful insights about your mortgage. For instance it will help you understand how to save money by pre-paying part of the principal amount.

How to use this tool

Input your mortgage data: principal amount, duration, start date (month/year), and annual interest rate. Summary and amortization schedule table will populate with the result.

You can optionally add one or more pre-payments to see how they will impact the amount of interest your need to pay.

Click on "Generate link" to save your results as a link that you can send via chat or email.

Privacy

This tool is completely self contained and doesn't share any of your data.
